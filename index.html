SoundRigMusic is a blockchain-based music platform aimed at empowering independent, upcoming, and undiscovered music artists. It focuses on connecting artists directly with fans, enabling fair compensation through encrypted, high-quality music distribution.

The platform provides tools for artists to monetize their work and create exclusive fan experiences, emphasizing a more equitable music industry. Additionally, SoundRig supports initiatives like #ProjectSIMBA, promoting social impact, and hosts a podcast, Sound of the Future, to showcase emerging talent.

Bishopfinance is a cryptocurrency project on the Sui blockchain, focused on SocialFi (social finance) within the Web3 ecosystem. It aims to reward users with its native $BHP token for social interactions like liking, commenting, and creating content, unlike traditional Web2 platforms where such actions go unrewarded.

The project emphasizes educating users about the Sui ecosystem and onboarding new participants through its "Bishop movement." Recent updates suggest that mining or earning $BHP tokens is becoming easier, indicating ongoing development to enhance user accessibility and engagement. The platform is designed to incentivize activity and rank users for higher earnings, fostering a community-driven approach to decentralized finance.
